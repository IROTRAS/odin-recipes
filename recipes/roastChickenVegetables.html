Roast Chicken and Vegetables

See full recipe here

Great chicken for to use all those vegetables from the garden. Feel free to experiment with different vegetables. Try different cuts of meat, just adjusting the cooking times.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1⁄2 lb fingerling potatoes scrubbed & cut into wedges
  • 1 ½ lb sweet potatoes scrubbed & cut in wedges
  • 2 shallots trimmed & peeled
  • 3 bunches of baby carrots trimmed & scrubbed
  • 1 fl oz olive oil
  • Dried rosemary
  • 3lb 5 oz chicken thighs
  • 2 shallots trimmed & peeled

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 335°F
  2. Toss vegetables, oil and rosemary in bowl. Divide between to 9x11 pans. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Season the chicken with salt and pepper and place chicken thighs over the top of the vegetables. Roast for 40 minutes, or until vegetables are tender and chicken is cooked.
  4. To serve, divide chicken and vegetables between serving plates and top with fresh parsley.
  5. Enjoy!
