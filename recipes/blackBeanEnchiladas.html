Black Bean Enchiladas

See full recipe here

Simple and delicious black bean enchilada recipe. Great meal for any time of year.

Ingredients:

  • olive oil spray
  • 3 tsp olive oil
  • 1 red bell peppers deseeded & diced
  • 3 garlic cloves minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 6-8 oz tomato sauce (small can)
  • Two big scoops of Pace Salsa
  • 2 can of black beans (14 oz cans) drained & rinsed
  • 3 tbs (2 fl oz) lime juice
  • salt & pepper, to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat an oven 350 deg and spray a 8x11 baking dish with oil.
  2. Heat oil in a large non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add onion to the skillet with the bell pepper. Cook, stirring, for 10 minutes until soft.
  3. At the same time combine salsa ingredients with other onion. Season with salt and pepper and set aside.
  4. Add garlic to cooked onion and cook for a minute until fragrant. Careful not to burn!
  5. Add spice, tomato sauce, salsa and black beans and season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 2-3 minutes until sauce thickens. Remove from heat and adjust seasoning.
  6. Put cheese in each tortilla, then spoon bean mixture over cheese. Roll and place seam-side down in baking dish. Scatter over cheese and place in oven about 20-25 mins. Until cheese melts and gets a little brown.
  7. Divide enchiladas between plates and serve topped with salsa.
  8. Enjoy!
